Kalki 2898 AD is a 2024 Indian Telugu-language epic science fiction film directed by Nag Ashwin and produced by Vyjayanthi Movies. The film features notable actors such as Prabhas, Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Haasan, and Deepika Padukone. It will be released in multiple languages, including Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada.
